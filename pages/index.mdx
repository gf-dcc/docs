The Gray Foundation Data Coordinating Center (GF-DCC) provide these documents to help researchers interact with the portal, data, metadata, and other resources. 

#### Mission
The [Gray foundation](https://www.grayfoundation.org) is focused on accelerating cancer research to study BRCA-associated precancer and early cancerous lesions with the hope of developing new prevention, early detection, and interception approaches. Proposals are solicited annually to fund BRCA-gene related research. Awardees of the Gray Foundation are encouraged to collaborate together.

#### Data Coordinating Center
Sage Bionetworks serves as the Data Coordinating Center (DCC) for the Gray Foundation consortium.
